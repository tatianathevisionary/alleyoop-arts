THE EXHIBIT

Basketball, hung like it matters.

Music got its album covers. Film got its one-sheets. Basketball got highlight clips that disappear in a day. We think the game deserves walls.

The Motion Gallery is Canada's first digital basketball motion-art gallery: living artworks built in 3D — every ball a real basketball, every shot a real arc, every loop seamless. Watch them here. Hang the stills at home. The prints are numbered, signed, and gone when they're gone.

WALL TEXT
An alley-oop is the one play you cannot run alone. Someone reads the floor. Someone trusts the lob. Someone finishes. Everything in this room is that pass — thrown to the athletes the highlight reels missed, the artists the galleries haven't found yet, and the kid still out there when the streetlights come on. The pass is the play.
