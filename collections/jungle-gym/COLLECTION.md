# THE JUNGLE GYM — Collection 02 · 20 Works

The playground as the first arena. Before the gym, before the coach, before anyone
was watching — there was a dome climber, a too-short rim, and a kid who wouldn't
come inside. This collection is for them.

Companion 3D piece: `3d/jungle-gym.html`. Palettes per `ALLEYOOP_ARTS_STYLE_GUIDE.md`
(hex-first, 3–5 colors, colors tied to objects). Ball and rim are always `#EE9933`.

Status: DRAFT — titles and prompts for Damion's review before any generation run.

---

## The works

### 01 · First Arena
*Nobody's first court had hardwood.*
> Poster artwork of an empty playground at dawn, dome climber and monkey bars in silhouette, a single basketball resting at center. Palette: #2B5D4B (climber structure), #88BBCC (dawn air), #EE9933 (the ball), #F2E9C7 (sky haze), #223333 (long shadows). Quiet, monumental, wall-art negative space.

### 02 · The Dome
*Half climbing frame, half cathedral.*
> Low-angle poster artwork looking up through the bars of a geodesic dome climber at the sky, a basketball wedged between bars glowing in the light. Palette: #2B5D4B (bars), #88BBCC (sky through the lattice), #EE9933 (wedged ball), #F2E9C7 (sun flare). Geometric, almost stained-glass composition.

### 03 · Monkey Bar Crossover
*The first handle anyone learns is hand over hand.*
> Poster artwork of a kid swinging across monkey bars with a basketball tucked under one arm, mid-swing, determined. Palette: #2B5D4B (bars), #40815D (rubber ground), #EE9933 (ball), #F2E9C7 (shirt highlight), #223333 (shadow). Dynamic diagonal composition.

### 04 · The Short Hoop
*Eight feet tall and it made more players than any arena.*
> Poster artwork of a lowered playground hoop with a worn net, a small ball mid-arc toward it, golden hour. Palette: #40815D (court), #2B5D4B (pole and fence), #EE9933 (rim and ball), #E0EBBF (warm sunlight). Tender, glowing, the rim slightly oversized in frame.

### 05 · Recess Royalty
*Fifteen minutes. Whole kingdoms rose and fell.*
> Poster artwork of kids mid-game on a playground court, rendered as bold color-block silhouettes, one player crowned by the sun's halo. Palette: #88BBCC (sky), #EE9933 (ball and energy accents), #2B5D4B (structures), #FF4FA3 (one pop accent), #F2E9C7 (light). Celebratory, graphic.

### 06 · Before the Whistle
*No refs. The arguments WERE the rules.*
> Poster artwork of two kids face to face under a playground hoop, ball held between them, classic standoff, exaggerated long shadows. Palette: #40815D (court green), #2B5D4B (fence), #EE9933 (ball at center), #223333 (shadow play), #F2E9C7 (highlights). Humor in the body language.

### 07 · Swing Set Skyline
*From the top of the arc you could see the whole world.*
> Poster artwork of a swing set silhouetted against a vast sky, one swing flying high, a basketball court tiny below. Palette: #88BBCC (huge sky), #2B5D4B (swing frame), #EE9933 (ball on the court below), #F2E9C7 (clouds). Scale contrast, wide-open composition.

### 08 · Gravel Handles
*Learn to dribble on gravel and concrete feels like glass.*
> Close-up poster artwork of a basketball bouncing on rough gravel, dust kicking up, pebble texture of ball against pebble texture of ground. Palette: #E09432 (ochre dust), #64714D (olive ground), #EE9933 (ball), #333D38 (deep shadow). Texture-on-texture, macro drama. (Autumn Court palette.)

### 09 · Caught in the Lattice
*The ball always found the one gap you couldn't reach.*
> Poster artwork of a basketball stuck high inside a jungle gym lattice, three kids below mid-scheme — one climbing, one pointing, one laughing. Palette: #2B5D4B (lattice), #88BBCC (air), #EE9933 (the prize), #F2E9C7 (light), #223333 (shadows). Storytelling piece.

### 10 · Tag, You're Defense
*Every playground game was secretly basketball training.*
> Poster artwork of kids playing tag through a maze of playground equipment, motion lines and bold shapes, one carrying a basketball. Palette: #88BBCC (sky), #40815D (ground), #EE9933 (ball and motion accents), #2B5D4B (equipment). Playful, kinetic, comic-panel energy.

### 11 · Sundown Game
*Five more minutes. Always five more minutes.*
> Poster artwork of a playground court at deep golden hour, kids as long-shadow silhouettes mid-game, parents waiting at the fence. Palette: #E09432 (ochre light), #333D38 (silhouettes), #EE9933 (ball), #F2E9C7 (sun haze), #64714D (olive edges). Warm, elegiac. (Autumn Court palette.)

### 12 · Shoes Over the Wire
*Somebody's brother said it means a player lives here.*
> Poster artwork of sneakers hanging from a power line above a playground court, hoop and dome climber below, evening sky. Palette: #251E4A (dusk violet), #3A6DB0 (electric blue haze), #E2726D (neon coral glow on the wire), #EE9933 (court rim below). Neon Night palette, mythic mood.

### 13 · Climbing Season
*The first vertical anyone ever worked on.*
> Poster artwork of one kid at the very top of the dome climber, arms up, sky huge around them, ball balanced on the crown bar beside them. Palette: #88BBCC (sky), #2B5D4B (dome), #EE9933 (ball), #F2E9C7 (clouds and light). Triumph framed small against big air.

### 14 · The Fence Watchers
*Every playground has a panel of unpaid analysts.*
> Poster artwork of a row of kids hanging on a chain-link fence watching a game, fingers hooked in the wire, rendered from inside the court. Palette: #40815D (court), #2B5D4B (fence posts), #EE9933 (ball in foreground), #223333 (fence shadow lattice), #F2E9C7 (highlights). The crowd as the subject.

### 15 · Juice Box Timeout
*Hydration strategy, age seven.*
> Poster artwork of three kids sitting on a painted court line, juice boxes in hand, ball resting between them, equipment looming behind like skyline. Palette: #40815D (court), #88BBCC (sky), #EE9933 (ball), #F2E9C7 (paper-bright juice boxes), #2B5D4B (equipment). Tender, funny, still-life calm.

### 16 · Rain Check
*The court remembers every game it ever held.*
> Poster artwork of an empty playground court in rain, hoop reflected in a huge puddle, dome climber blurred behind the weather. Palette: #223333 (wet ground), #88BBCC (rain air), #2B5D4B (structures), #EE9933 (rim doubled in reflection). Moody mirror composition.

### 17 · King of the Court, Age 9
*The crown was invisible. Everyone could see it.*
> Poster artwork portrait of a kid holding a basketball under one arm at the playground gate, low hero angle, equipment kingdom behind them. Palette: #88BBCC (sky), #2B5D4B (kingdom), #EE9933 (ball), #F2E9C7 (rim light), #223333 (grounding shadow). Portrait-format hero shot.

### 18 · The Lattice Lob
*Thread it through the bars. House rules.*
> Poster artwork of a basketball mid-flight threading through a gap in the jungle gym toward the short hoop beyond, trajectory drawn as a thin arc. Palette: #2B5D4B (lattice), #88BBCC (air), #EE9933 (ball and arc), #F2E9C7 (light), #40815D (ground). The signature alley-oop geometry, playground edition.

### 19 · Graffiti Recess
*Somebody's older cousin made the wall beautiful.*
> Poster artwork of a playground wall covered in bold graffiti shapes, kids playing ball in front, art and game as one scene. Palette: #FF4FA3 (hot magenta wall blocks), #0A85BB (spray cyan), #F49B48 (tangerine), #413C44 (asphalt), #F2E9C7 (paper base). Graffiti Pop palette, high-contrast daylight.

### 20 · Last Light Layup
*One more. Then one more. Then the streetlights won.*
> Poster artwork of a single kid finishing a layup on the short hoop as streetlights flicker on, the playground equipment going to silhouette, first stars out. Palette: #251E4A (midnight violet), #3A6DB0 (electric blue), #E2726D (streetlight glow), #EE9933 (ball and rim), #0B0B14 (near-black). Neon Night palette. The collection's closing image.

## Series logic

- **Arc:** dawn (01) → day (02–10) → golden hour (11) → dusk (12) → night (20). Hang them in order and the collection is one day on the playground — every basketball life's first day.
- **Drop plan:** Drop 02, edition of 50 each, numbered and signed. *Limited possessions.*
- **Cross-sell:** pairs with the Jungle Gym motion piece (`3d/jungle-gym.html`) as the product-page hero; "Last Light Layup" pairs with the Neon Court loop.
