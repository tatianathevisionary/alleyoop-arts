## The campaign idea

**Basketball, hung like it matters.**

One claim carries the launch: this is Canada's first digital basketball motion-art
gallery. ⚠️ *Verify the "first" claim before any paid placement — a competitor search
is one afternoon and the claim is the spine of the campaign.*

Three-act rollout:
1. **THE TEASE (week 1)** — loops with no explanation. The galaxy, the neon court. Caption: a single line. No brand push.
2. **THE REVEAL (week 2)** — the brand intro runs as the first "TV ad." The exhibit page opens. Press line goes out.
3. **THE DROP (week 3)** — Drop 01 prints on the store. "Limited possessions." Jungle Gym collection teased as Drop 02.

## TV / pre-roll ad scripts

### 30s — "The First Arena" (hero spot)
| Time | Visual | Audio |
|------|--------|-------|
| 0–3 | Black. One bounce echoes. | Single ball bounce, gym reverb |
| 3–8 | THE SHOT loop: dribble, dribble, the arc, the swish | Real ball sounds only |
| 8–14 | MAZE RUN: the ball dribbling hard corners, finishing at the rim | Bounces tighten in rhythm |
| 14–20 | BASKETBALL GALAXY: the seamed planet, words orbiting — THE · PASS · IS · THE · PLAY | Sound falls away to a low hum |
| 20–26 | Hard cut to cream. The lockup assembles: ALLEYOOP, double-O rims, the orange arc | One warm note |
| 26–30 | Hold. "THE MOTION GALLERY — OPEN NOW." URL. | Silence, then one last bounce |

### 15s — cutdown
THE SHOT (4s) → GALAXY with orbiting words (5s) → lockup + "THE GALLERY FOR THE GAME — NOW IN MOTION" + URL (6s).

### 6s — bumper
Swish through the neon net (3s) → lockup card + one line: "GET POSTERIZED." (3s).

Sound rule across all spots: the game's real sounds, one musical note, no music bed.
The restraint is the brand.
