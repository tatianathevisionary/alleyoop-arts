## 1. Executive Summary

Alleyoop is a Canadian non-profit cultural organization that elevates basketball as
art, culture, and storytelling. Founded by Damion Rashford — former college basketball
player, now a builder working with Shopify's developer ecosystem and creating
open-source AI tools — it produces a premium digital magazine, limited-edition art
prints, and cultural experiences celebrating Canadian athletes, artists, and voices.

**Core offerings:**
- Digital magazine (site-first) → annual print edition
- Shopify store: limited art prints, magazine, select merch
- Events, exhibitions, community programs

**Model:** hybrid non-profit — earned revenue (art sales + subscriptions) +
sponsorships + grants/donations. Earned revenue keeps quality independent of grant
cycles; non-profit status makes sponsorship a partnership, not ad buying.

**Positioning:** Canada's premium platform for basketball culture, with a built-in
path to global relevance.

**Head start most plans don't have:** a catalog of 156 original artworks and two
production-ready FLUX generation pipelines already exist in this repo. Product
inventory is a curation problem, not a creation problem.

## 5. Market Opportunity

Canadian basketball culture is growing fast; no premium, artistically driven national
platform serves it. Existing outlets (BasketballBuzz, SLAM-style coverage) are
news/stats/lifestyle-first. The gap: high-end photography, illustration, long-form
storytelling, and collectible art.

**Drivers:** rising Canadian participation and talent pipeline · sponsorship dollars
shifting from sports media to culture · demand for limited-edition cultural products ·
no design-forward voice in the space.

**Audience:** basketball fans who value culture and design · creatives and young
professionals (18–35) · athletes, coaches, families · brands seeking cultural
partnerships.

*(Caveat: market claims above are directional and should be backed with citable
numbers — participation stats, media landscape audit — before appearing in any
sponsor-facing document.)*
